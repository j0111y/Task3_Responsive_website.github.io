Hobbies

Crocheting

Crochet is my beloved hobby, a creative and relaxing craft that brings joy to my life. With a simple hook and yarn, I delight in transforming threads into beautiful and intricate patterns. Crocheting allows me to unwind and express my artistic side, while also gifting me the satisfaction of creating handmade pieces for myself and my loved ones.

Programming

Programming is my passion and a fulfilling pursuit that fuels my curiosity and ambition. With a keyboard and code, I immerse myself in the world of logical problem-solving and algorithmic thinking. Programming empowers me to bring ideas to life, crafting software and applications that have real-world impact. As I dive into the intricacies of coding languages, I find joy in the challenge of overcoming obstacles and refining my skills. Whether it's developing innovative projects, automating tasks, or building interactive websites, programming ignites my creativity and fuels my desire to constantly learn and grow in this ever-evolving field.

Drawing

Drawing is my ultimate passion and a cherished escape that allows me to express myself freely. Armed with pencils and colors, I lose track of time as I create captivating illustrations and bring imagination to life on paper. With each stroke, I find solace and a profound sense of accomplishment, witnessing my art evolve with every new piece. Drawing is more than just a hobby; it's a soulful connection to my inner creativity, providing me endless inspiration and moments of pure joy.

About

During my academic journey in Business Informatics, I have been introduced to Python, where I am in the process of learning and gaining familiarity with the language. While I'm still in the early stages of my Python journey, I have had the opportunity to explore its basics and understand fundamental concepts. As part of my coursework, I engaged in Python exercises and simple projects, which have provided valuable hands-on experience. Additionally, I have taken introductory courses on SQL to understand database querying. Recently, I began learning HTML and CSS through SoloLearn, a step towards expanding my skill set and exploring the world of web development.
